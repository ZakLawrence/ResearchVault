The **covariance** of two random variables $X$ and $Y$ is a measure of the **joint variability** of two variables. It is defined as the [[Expectation Value]] of the product of the [[Variance]].
$$\text{cov}(X,Y) = <(x-<x>)(y-<y>)> $$ 
$$= <xy - x<y> - y<x>+<x><y>> $$
$$= <xy>-<x><y>$$
